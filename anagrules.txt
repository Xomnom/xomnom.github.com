Anagrams XChat script game instructions by Xomnom
  last modified: 2010 Feb. 25

To start a game, type:

  !anag

To stop, type:

  !quit

To claim a word, e.g. 'word', from the free tiles, type (case-insensitive):

  =word

To claim a word, e.g. 'world', from a combination of words and free tiles, type:

  =world word

Each word is worth the sum of its letters' Scrabble values plus the square of three less than its length.
The dictionary contains words between four and fifteen letters, inclusive.
Wildcards and ten random tiles are omitted from each game.
